Our Services

//////////

As a transactional law firm, FixLex lawyers know the markets as well as the law. We understand well the political, economic and cultural factors at play, and the way national and international regulators operate and cooperate. We focus on the following practice areas:

CORPORATE & COMMERCIAL

CORPORATE & COMMERCIAL

Our practice is centered on transactional and legal advisory services concerning day-to-day business, regulatory issues and corporate governance affairs. We take care of the legal aspect of all kinds of commercial transactions. We also help our clients establish their businesses in Rwanda and take care of their legal issues throughout their business journey. We use multidisciplinary approach to advise our clients on legal issues concerning commercial transactions, restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, formation of joint ventures and we keep advising them through all stages of their complex assignments. FixLex aims not to be only your legal adviser, but to stand shoulder to shoulder in all your business legal requirements.

BANKING AND FINANCE

Lenders and borrowers face a lot of challenges, including legal and business related. The good news is that some of our Attorneys have been bankers themselves. With our experience representing lenders and borrowers, and handling other different banking and finance transactions, FixLex is exceptionally well positioned to help you close all your banking and finance transactions.

CAPITAL MARKET

We offer comprehensive legal support on a variety of offerings in the domestic and international capital markets. Domestically we represent and advise entities that issue securities to raise capital and security holders seeking to sell their securities. Our Attorneys have comprehensive understanding of the national securities laws administered by Rwanda Capital Market Authority as well as the rules of Rwanda Stock Exchange.

REAL ESTATE

We advise clients with regard to the entire spectrum of their real estate-related needs, from land acquisition and use to development and construction, acquisitions, dispositions, financing and leasing as well as real estate management. Our clients benefit from other practice areas of our firm on matters involving construction, environmental, tax, litigation, bankruptcy and finance.

TAX

Our Attorneys have handled some of the most complex tax issues at other law firms and other business places where they worked before. There is no doubt that the depth and breadth of our legal and market knowledge guarantees our capabilities to support your business with advanced tax solutions in the increasingly challenging business environment. We advise a wide spectrum of clients on tax, whether private sector, institutional and multi-national organizations as well as foreign investors.

Jean Pierre TUYISHIME

Practical Manager

Lorem ipsum dolor Jean Pierre is an Advocate of the High Court of Rwanda who practices under FixLex Law Chambers as Principal Attorney and Practice Manager. Prior to joining FixLex Law Chambers, He served at CERTA LAW as Attorney. Before the Call to the Bar, Jean Pierre served as Senior Legal Officer at EQUITY BANK RWANDA PLC and beforehand was an in-house lawyer at ACCESS BANK RWANDA PLC. Besides legal practice, He teaches Law at various Universities in Rwanda and is a Fellow of the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI). Jean Pierre holds a Master’s Degree in Law (LLM) from the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania where he focused on East African Community Law and Law of Regional Economic Integration. He is a holder of a Postgraduate Diploma in Legal Practice (DLP) from ILPD and a First Class Bachelor’s Degree in Law (LLB) from Kigali Independent University, Rwanda. His practice areas include corporate and commercial, banking and finance, tax, capital market, as well as real estate.
